# grail
AI processed corpus of Hermetic Knowledge


Inspired by the documentary "Embassy of the Free Mind", 
this project envisions to enrich a LLM with Hermetic Knowledge collected from historical and contemporary sources from all over the world  including Taoism, Sufism, Freemasonry, Mysticism, Gnosis, Hermetic Philosophy, Alchemy, Theosophy, Folklore, etc, etc, in order to reduce boundaries of language and clarify interpretational understanding of symbology facilitating a comparative study of shared wisdom that unites humanity.
